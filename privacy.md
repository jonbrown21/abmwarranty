## Privacy

ABM Warranty is designed for operational use and reads authorized management data to present warranty and device status clearly.

### Data handling
- No broad device tracking beyond authenticated management connections.
- Data is used only for app functionality and reporting.
- You can remove local data and reconnect as needed.
