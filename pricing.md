## ABM Warranty Pricing

### Core plan details

- **Free**: up to 3 tenants.
- **MSP / Consultancy**: $19.99 per month for 3+ tenants.
- **Support add-on**: available for both tiers and delivered via Zoom or phone.

### Why support exists

Support is not required for every user. It is available for teams that need guided setup, migration support, or operational help during rollout.
